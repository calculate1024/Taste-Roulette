## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Tired of hearing the same 50 songs on repeat?

Taste Roulette is a music discovery app built on one idea: the best recommendations come from real people, not algorithms.

Every day, you receive one song — chosen by a real person whose taste is deliberately different from yours. Not random. Not AI-generated. A genuine human pick, matched to you based on "taste distance."

HOW IT WORKS

1. Complete a quick music taste quiz (takes 2 minutes)
2. We build your taste profile — a 21-dimension genre map
3. Every day at 8 PM, you get one card: a song from a stranger
4. Listen, react (Surprised / Okay / Not for me)
5. Then recommend a song back to another stranger

THE ANTI-ALGORITHM APPROACH

Most music apps want to give you more of what you already like. Taste Roulette does the opposite — it finds the sweet spot between "different enough to surprise you" and "not so far you'll hate it."

We calculate the taste distance between you and your recommender. Too close means no surprise. Too far means rejection. The magic happens in the middle.

FEATURES

- Daily roulette card with Spotify playback
- Anonymous recommendations (you see taste labels like "Jazz Explorer", not real names)
- Taste radar chart showing your genre profile evolution
- Streak tracking and discovery badges
- Bookmark songs you want to revisit
- Recommend-back: share your own discoveries with strangers

WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT

- One card per day. No infinite feed. No doom scrolling.
- Real people, not algorithms. Every recommendation is human-curated.
- Anonymous but personal. Taste labels give personality without identity.
- Built for surprise. We optimize for "I never would have found this" moments.

Free. No ads. No premium tier. Built as a passion project by someone who believes music discovery should feel like serendipity, not surveillance.
