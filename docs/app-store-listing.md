## Description

Every day, you receive one music recommendation — from a stranger whose taste is nothing like yours.

No algorithm. No infinite feed. No echo chamber. Just one surprise.

HOW IT WORKS
• Complete a quick taste profile (swipe through songs)
• Every day at 8 PM, you get a card with a song picked by a stranger
• The song is chosen to surprise you — not to match your taste
• Listen, react, and discover something new

RECOMMEND BACK
After hearing your daily surprise, recommend a song to another stranger. Your pick might become someone else's favorite discovery.

TASTE JOURNEY
Watch your musical comfort zone expand. Track which genres you've explored, earn badges for adventurous listening, and see how far from your usual taste you've traveled.

ANONYMOUS & HUMAN
Every recommendation comes from a real person, identified only by their taste label — "Jazz Curious," "Metal Warrior," "Indie Dreamer." No names, no profiles, just music.

WHY TASTE ROULETTE?
In a world of algorithmic echo chambers, we believe the best discoveries come from humans, not machines. Taste Roulette is the anti-algorithm — designed to push you out of your comfort zone, one song at a time.

Free. No ads. No data selling.
