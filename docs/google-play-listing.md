## Full Description

You already know what you like. Taste Roulette shows you what you're missing.

Every day, you get one music recommendation — not from a playlist, not from a machine trained on your listening history, but from a real person whose taste is genuinely different from yours. Not so different that you'd skip it immediately. Just different enough to surprise you.

That's it. One card. One song. Every day.

HOW IT WORKS

When you join, Taste Roulette learns your taste through a short swipe quiz — a handful of tracks across genres, and you react: love it, it's okay, or not for me. That builds your taste profile.

Then the matching begins. Someone out there — whose musical world doesn't quite overlap with yours — has submitted a recommendation into the pool. The app finds the sweet spot: far enough to stretch your ears, close enough that you won't hate it. You get their pick, with a single sentence explaining why they thought of it.

You never know their name. You see a label: "Soul Collector." "Noise Punk Guy." "Cumbia Obsessive." Just their taste, distilled.

After you listen, leave a quick reaction — Surprised, It's Fine, or Not For Me. Then, if you want, flip it around: recommend something back. Search for a track and write one sentence about why someone else should hear it. Your pick goes into the pool for a stranger somewhere to receive tomorrow.

KEY FEATURES

One recommendation per day — no infinite scroll, no rabbit holes, just one intentional listen.

Taste distance matching — recommendations come from people whose profile sits in a "surprise sweet spot": different enough to be interesting, similar enough to be listenable.

Anonymous by design — no profiles, no follower counts, no social pressure. Just music and a taste label.

Recommend back — after receiving, you can submit your own pick into the pool. It's optional, but encouraged. When you do, you get a bonus card.

Taste Journey — watch your own musical range grow over time. A radar chart tracks which genres you've explored, how far you've ventured from your starting point, and which surprises actually landed.

Streaks and badges — light gamification for people who want it. Keep your daily streak, earn badges like "Five Genres Deep" or "First Surprise," or ignore all of it. The app works either way.

No ads. No premium paywall. No algorithmic feed trying to keep you scrolling.

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

Most recommendation systems optimize for engagement — they keep feeding you more of what you already respond to, because that's what keeps you in the app. Taste Roulette optimizes for the opposite: one moment per day where you encounter something genuinely unexpected, from a person who actually loves it.

It's less like a streaming service and more like a pen pal who has good taste and terrible taste, from your perspective — and that's exactly the point.

If you've ever discovered a song through a stranger at a party, or had a friend play you something you'd have never found yourself, and it stuck with you for years — that's what this tries to recreate. Deliberately. Daily.

Free to download. Free to use.
