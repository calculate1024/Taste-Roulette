# Product Hunt Listing Draft

## Tagline (60 chars)
One stranger. One song. Zero algorithms telling you what you already like.

## Description

Taste Roulette is a daily music recommendation app built on a simple premise: the best discoveries come from people who don't share your taste.

Every day you receive exactly one song — chosen by a stranger whose musical taste sits 30–70% away from yours. Not so different that it feels like noise, not so similar that it's just more of the same. That sweet spot is calculated by a "taste distance" algorithm that intentionally avoids the echo chamber. No infinite feed. No engagement loops. Just one card, once a day.

The twist: after you react to your card, you can recommend a song back to a different stranger — keeping the loop alive. Everyone stays anonymous, identified only by taste labels like "Jazz Lover" or "Indie Kid." You're not following anyone. You're just passing music through a chain of strangers with different ears.

Built as a solo side project, running on React Native + Supabase + Spotify Embed.

## Maker Comment

I got tired of every streaming app learning exactly what I like and then feeding it back to me forever. There's no discovery in that — just confirmation. I built Taste Roulette to scratch my own itch: get one song a day from someone whose taste is genuinely different from mine, with no pressure to rate, rank, or build a playlist out of it. It's been a surprisingly meditative habit. Android-first for now, iOS coming. Would love honest feedback from fellow music obsessives.
