## Content Rules

- **Stats posts**: Only use real data (no fabrication). Hold stats content until 20+ real users
- **Tone**: Curious, warm, slightly playful. A music-obsessed friend, not a marketing team
- **No**: "revolutionary", "game-changing", excessive exclamation marks
- **Hashtags**: Use 2-3 per post: `#TasteRoulette` `#AntiAlgorithm` `#MusicDiscovery`
- **Beta link**: Include Google Play internal test link in bio, not in every post
- **User quotes**: Get consent before quoting, even anonymously
- **Song posts**: Text only or Spotify embed, no unlicensed clips
